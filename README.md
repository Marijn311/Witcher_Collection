I find it a shame that adult books rarely include illustrations. There is a lot of excellent fan art that stays true to the books and is enjoyable to view while reading.

After finishing a chapter, I like to treat myself to illustrations depicting the events I have just read about. Searching for this kind of content online takes effort and often exposes you to spoilers.

That is why I am creating **section 5**, which contains book-accurate illustrations of the events of each chapter. The goal is to let you experience *The Witcher* books as if they were fully illustrated.
